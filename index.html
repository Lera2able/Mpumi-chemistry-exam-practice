Exam Guidelines

These guidelines are based on the CAPS curriculum and the 2024 exam structure.

Paper Structure

Paper 2: Chemistry - 150 marks, 3 hours

  • 10 multiple-choice questions (20 marks)
  • Structured questions (130 marks)

Marking Guidelines

Understanding how marks are allocated can help you maximize your score:

  • Show ALL formulae and substitutions in calculations
  • Round off final answers to TWO decimal places
  • Write neatly and legibly
  • Number answers correctly according to the question numbering

Key Topics

The Chemistry paper covers these main areas:

  • Organic Chemistry
  • Energy and Change
  • Rate and Extent of Reaction
  • Chemical Equilibrium
  • Acids and Bases
  • Electrochemical Reactions

Useful Tips

  • Start each question on a new page in your answer book
  • Leave one line between two subquestions
  • You may use a non-programmable calculator
  • Refer to the DATA SHEETS provided during the exam
